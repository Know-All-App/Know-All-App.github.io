Privacy Policy


Thank you for using Know All, a mobile application designed to help parents answer their children's questions using artificial intelligence technology. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. By using this app, you agree to the following Privacy Policy:


Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Know All:

- Log Information: We may collect email and log information when you use our app, app usage data, and crash reports.

- User Content: We may collect the answers that you added to your favorite in order you can revisit them.


How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our app's service, including:

- Answering your questions with artificial intelligence technology.

- Analyzing app usage data to improve the app's functionality and user experience.

- Preventing fraudulent or illegal activities.


Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us provide and improve our app's service. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.


Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.


Children's Privacy

Know All is not intended for use by children under the age of 14, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 14 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove the information and terminate the child's account.


Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on our app and update the effective date. Your continued use of the app after the updated Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at knowall-app@hotmail.com .
